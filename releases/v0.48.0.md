# v0.48.0

Gateway-mode hardening lands as a cohesive surface: gateway-mode towers
now refuse direct Dolt sync/push/pull, route every store mutation
through the gateway or fail closed, carry archmage identity (`X-Archmage-Name`/`X-Archmage-Email`)
through `/api/v1/*` mutations onto bead labels and dolt commit authors,
expose a soft-reset endpoint, and dispatch summon/ready/close on tower
deployment mode rather than direct kubectl probes. The cluster-attach
direction converges: bidirectional cluster sync is disabled â€” GCS is
the archive/DR path â€” and backup defaults to enabled with fail-fast
validation. The wizardregistry epic replaces `pkg/registry` with a
mode-portable adapter (`pkg/wizardregistry`) backed by local zombie-safe
liveness and a k8s pod-backed cluster impl; AgentMonitor and OrphanSweep
flow through the new contract. Reset gains real teeth: it terminates
bead-scoped child and recovery processes, rebinds run-scoped workspaces,
and normalizes abandoned active predecessors on `--to --force`. Steward
timing decouples from the daemon interval, runs by default in `spire up`,
gates OrphanSweep on attempt heartbeat freshness, and uses heartbeat as
the timeout clock. A new `/api/v1/beads/{id}/trace` endpoint exposes
bead-graph trace data.

## Features

- **Gateway-mode tower hardening** (`spi-zz2ve9`, `spi-i7k1ag.4`, `spi-hr3tcv`, `spi-gg2y2j`, `spi-n6fk2h`) â€” gateway-mode towers now route every store mutation through the gateway or fail closed; direct Dolt sync/push/pull are rejected; `POST /api/v1/beads/{id}/reset` (soft) lands; archmage identity (`X-Archmage-Name`/`X-Archmage-Email`) threads through every `/api/v1/*` mutation onto bead labels (`archmage:<name>`) and dolt commit authors. - **wizardregistry: unified liveness + ownership across local/cluster** (`spi-hbk9yi`, `spi-p6unf3`, `spi-w60her`, `spi-bsr4sj`, `spi-gm7t2u`, `spi-o1veri`) â€” `pkg/wizardregistry` defines a contract + conformance suite + fake impl; `pkg/wizardregistry/local` ships zombie-safe `IsAlive`; `pkg/wizardregistry/cluster` is a k8s pod-backed impl; AgentMonitor + OrphanSweep migrate to the new registry, and `pkg/registry` is removed. One liveness story whether the wizard is a local PID or a cluster pod.
- **Cluster-as-truth direction** (`spi-q5q6s6`, `spi-6az9s7`, `spi-i7k1ag.1`, `spi-i7k1ag.2`) â€” bidirectional cluster syncer disabled; GCS is the archive/DR path. `backup.enabled=true` is the default with fail-fast validation.
